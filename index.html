🏥 About ESV ICD-11 Smart Tool


ESV ICD-11 Smart Tool is an online and offline version for disease coding and classification system designed for:

  • HMIS Officers
  • Clinicians
  • anyone who wants to know about the structure of ICD-11

Contains more than 1400 diseases, organized into 26 ICD-11 chapters.

Developed by Anmut Tsegaye (BSC in Health Informatics).
